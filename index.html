Process

How this archive is built

Every night, new documents are found, read, and examined. Here is the process, start to finish.

Search
8 open-access databases scanned for new strategy documents and evaluation reports.
Extract
Full text extracted from PDFs and web pages. Up to 30 pages per document.
Identify
Direct quotes are identified where organisations make commitments across five categories.
Review
Each quote is challenged: is it a real commitment or aspirational language? Scored 1–5. Below 3 is removed.
Archive
Surviving quotes, summaries, and visualisations enter the archive. Flagged for editorial review.

All quotes are exact extracts from source documents, not paraphrases. If something looks wrong, the source link is there.
